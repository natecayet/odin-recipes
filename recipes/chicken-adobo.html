Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken Adobo is a simple but delicious filipino dish. It is chicken marinated in soysauce and a little vinegar then braised in it's own marinade. It's tender, savory, tangy and traditionally paired with white rice.

Ingredients

  • 5 cuts of chicken (thighs or drumsicks or mixed)
  • 1 cup soysauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar (or applecider)
  • 1pc bayleaf
  • 1/2 tbsp black peppercorn
  • pinch of salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken with clean water.
  2. Combine all ingradients except the water in a ziplock bag or a bowl and marinate for 1 hour or overnight.
  3. In a sauce pan, heat oil and sear chicken on all sides until it shows some color. Set the marinade aside.
  4. Once chicken is seared properly, pour in the marinade and add water until it covers the chicken halfway. Bring to a boil then down to a simmer. Cover with a lid and cook for 1 hour or until chicken is tender.
  5. Taste the flavor of the sauce and add a pinch of salt to taste if needed.
