Roasted Tomato Soup

Description

Warm up your day with a deliciuos Roasted Tomato Soup. Pair it with artisan toast or garlic bread and experience a world of umami flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs roma tomatoes (cut in half)
  • 1 large onion (quarter slices)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp chicken base (or 1 chicken cube)
  • 1 liter water
  • 1/2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tbsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to tatse

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450F. Place the tomatoes, garlic, and onions on a sheet pan.
  2. Drizzle them with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper then place in the oven and roast for 30 minutes.
  3. Transfer the roasted tomatoes, garlic, and onions in a blender. Blend until smooth then transfer to a pot.
  4. Add the water and bring to a boil then mix in the chicken base, oregano, and basil.
  5. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed and finish with heavy cream.
