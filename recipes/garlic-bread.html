Garlic Bread

Description

An easy garlic butter scpread recipe that can transform any bread into a crispy, garlicy, french delight.

Ingredients

  • sliced artisan bread
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tbsp oregano
  • ground black pepper (to taste)
  • salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400F. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients except the bread.
  2. Spread the garlic butter on the bread and place it on a sheet pan, butter side up.
  3. Place in the oven to toast for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
